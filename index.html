Approach

Standard slot development copies proven formulas. I work in the opposite direction — starting from mathematical structures that produce emergent behavior, then finding game expressions for those dynamics.

The result: mechanics that feel fresh because they arise from genuine complexity, not surface variation. Players encounter patterns they haven't seen, but the math underneath is rigorous and production-ready.
